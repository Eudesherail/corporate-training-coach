# Product Overview

Corporate Training Coach is a focused internal enablement platform for:

- HR onboarding
- SOP training
- compliance education
- internal policy and handbook support

## Primary User Flows

### Admin / Training Manager

- Upload onboarding handbooks, SOPs, policies, and compliance PDFs
- Organize documents by category
- Generate quizzes from uploaded documents
- Review employee roster and rollout readiness

### Employee

- Ask grounded questions against uploaded materials
- Receive document citations with page references
- Take quizzes generated from company materials
- Track onboarding and training completion

## Product Positioning

This is not a general chatbot. It is a document-grounded training assistant designed to reduce onboarding time and improve policy accuracy in day-to-day employee support.
